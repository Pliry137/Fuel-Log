Joe eats 2-3 meals, minimal snacking. Typical pattern:

**Morning (standard smoothie):**
- 10oz Wegmans High Protein Fat Free milk (80 cal / 14g protein per cup — fat free, ultra-filtered)
- 2 scoops Orgain Vegan Vanilla protein (160 cal / 21g protein per 2 scoops / 51g serving)

- 1 tbsp ground flaxseed

- ½ banana
- **Total: ~527 cal / 54g protein / 63g carbs / 16g fat**

**Midday snack (frequent):**
- Chobani Greek Yogurt 20g protein version — 140 cal / 20g protein / 8g carbs / 3g fat

**Dinner:** varies — see food history below

## Weekly patterns and observations
- **Smoothie days** hit 120-140g protein easily. Non-smoothie days struggle to break 110g.
- **Protein target** (168g) is the hardest metric to hit consistently. Ground beef and chicken are the main drivers when hit.
- **Calorie overages** happen primarily from pasta + ground beef dinners and Mac & cheese. Those meals run 800-950 cal.
- **Snack creep** (pretzels, dried mango, cheese) adds 200-300 cal quietly on most days.
- **Good snack anchors:** Chobani (140 cal / 20g P), beef sticks (90 cal / 9g P), bell pepper + hummus (65 cal).
- **Bad swaps:** Cheetos, granola — high cal, low protein payoff.
- Joe drinks 3-5L water daily. 2x Coke Zero — not logged, zero cal.
- Joe plays soccer usually once per week and does HIIT 4-5x/week mornings. Desk job rest of day.
- Joe has a Whoop — use actual burned calories from Whoop for deficit calculation when available.

## How to add food entries via API
When Joe shares a food photo or describes what he ate, parse the macros and POST to the local API:

```bash
curl -X POST http://localhost:3456/api/entries \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"date":"2026-06-02","time":"12:30","name":"Grilled Chicken (6 oz)","calories":278,"protein":53,"carbs":0,"fat":6}'
```

Or fetch the current day's entries:
```bash
curl http://localhost:3456/api/entries
```
